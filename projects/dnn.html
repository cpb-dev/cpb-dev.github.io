Using a Deep Neural Network to Produce Novel Music

Project Purpose:

The question of whether Artificial Intelligence can truly produce art, has been around for as long as the field of study itself. Art and music alike, are innately human, from the process to the creativity that goes into producing the final product. Now this project wasn't meant to crack the creative process and produce and Artificial Intelligence that could think of new original song ideas. Instead, it should be possible to find patterns within songs, either related by genre, artist or mood. The idea is to see what can be understood and recreated from such patterns, which would also be heavily influenced by the data gathered and it's file type.
